Why Oracle

There are many cloud providers:

Amazon Web Services (AWS) – The largest cloud provider / Microsoft Azure – A strong competitor to AWS, popular for enterprise solutions / Google Cloud Platform (GCP) – Known for its data analytics, AI/ML, and Kubernetes services / Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) – A growing player with a strong focus on enterprise workloads / IBM Cloud – Specializes in hybrid cloud, AI

I enjoy working with Oracle and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) because they offer robust, scalable solutions that empower me to design and manage complex data systems with precision. Oracle’s powerful database capabilities, combined with OCI’s flexible cloud services, allow me to optimize performance, ensure high availability, and tackle challenging technical problems.

I appreciate how OCI integrates seamlessly with modern architectures, enabling me to build secure, efficient, and innovative solutions that drive real business impact.
